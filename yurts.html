The Yurts at Pacitic Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxury yuts are permanent structures four feet off the ground Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts furniched?

Each yurt is furnshed with a queen-size bad with down quilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold runing water. Shower and restoomfacillites are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walkng shoes and plan to dress for changing weaher with layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlinght and a sense of adventure!
